## Creative Notes (All Ads)

- **Visuals:** Dark backgrounds (#141418) with lime (#D5EB4B) accent for data and numbers. Clean, minimal.
- **Font pairing:** Space Grotesk for headlines, Inter for body, JetBrains Mono for stats/numbers.
- **Numbers in visuals:** Always show ₹ amounts and percentages large. Founders respond to specific numbers.
- **No stock photos.** Data visuals, charts, comparisons, and text-driven creatives only.
- **Sizes:** 1080x1080 (feed) + 1080x1920 (story) for each creative.
- **Logo:** brand-assets/zippyscale-logo-for-dark-bg.png in bottom corner of every creative.
- **Video hooks (if running Reels/Stories):** First 2 seconds must show the pain stat or controversy. No logo intros.
