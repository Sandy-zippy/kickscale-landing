Why most campaigns actually fail

It's not the product. It's not the video. It's the unit economics. Here's the formula every builder should understand.

The Crowdfunding Money Equation

Every successful campaign comes down to one formula. If the math doesn't work at small scale, it won't work at large scale. Most agencies skip this step and go straight to spending your money.

// The formula
(VIPs x conversion_rate x AOV) > (ad_spend + fees)

If you can get this equation positive at 100 VIPs, you can get it positive at 10,000. If you can't, no amount of ad spend will save you.

FAQ

Common questions

What's included in the $4K test phase?

Everything you need to validate your campaign economics: landing page build, ad creatives, copy, and enough ad spend to get 100 VIP signups. You'll know your real cost-per-lead, engagement rates, and whether the math works before committing to a full launch.

What if the test phase numbers look bad?

That's the whole point. Better to find out at $4K than at $50K. If the economics don't work, we'll tell you straight - and you'll have real data on why. Sometimes it's fixable (pricing, positioning, creatives). Sometimes the market's telling you something. Either way, you'll know.

How is this different from LaunchBoom or Funded Today?

They charge $15-30K upfront and hope it works. We run a $4K test phase first. You get real conversion data before committing to a full launch budget. If the numbers don't work, you saved yourself a lot of money. If they do, we scale together.

What types of products do you work with?

Physical products only. Tech gadgets, consumer electronics, outdoor gear, home goods. No films, games, comics, or apps - the playbook is different for those categories and we're not the right fit.

How long does the test phase take?

Typically 2-3 weeks. A few days to build the funnel and creatives, then we run ads until we hit 100 VIPs. The timeline depends on your niche and how quickly we can acquire leads at a reasonable cost.
